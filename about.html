About

TEAM FW-LB

Team Flying Wing and Lifting Body, or simply Team FW-LB, started their journey under SSERD with a head full of questions and the will to contribute. We had the room to think, resources to use and a team of 24 to work with. There was nothing left to chance. Team FW-LB believes, anything and everything we do, contributes for that little something that revives faith of man in humanity and its endeavours. It has been this common ground that kept us resonating for the greater good and eventually for the better.
